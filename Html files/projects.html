PROJECT DETAILS

FOOD FEST MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Enhanced advertisement of Food Festivals within the universities. The website contains all the information about the Food Festivals including the Menu items, ticket price, stall info, and contact info about the stalls.

This is the first website on the web which provides easy management of Food Festivals in colleges. The project entitled “Food Fest Management System” Is aimed at maintained information about food stall’s about food stall mentors. This is a web application which facilitates the smooth interaction between food fest organizers and customers. This project clearly understands how the actual managing going on. This project includes the overall management of the food fest.
An introduction section promotes then descriptive representation of the project, and what is the project and how it works, it also gives clear picture of how the existing system works and its importance.
The main aim of “Food Fest Management System” is to control the food fest process well organized by institution. Food Fest Management System acts as bridges between food stall mentors and food fest customers. Food Fest Management System maintains the documentation of all the food stall and the queries generated for the food stall.
The web application is developed to focus on difficulty faced by the food fest organizers. The main aim of the project is to be in a social platform that brings to gather food fest organizers and food fest customers who are interested in food fest. Food Fest Management System provides the flexible platform to manage all food fest activities and connects individuals interested in food fest participation and eager to taste the different stall’s food. Finally, the project is very user friendly for both food fest organizers and food fest organizers. It had huge future enhancement.
